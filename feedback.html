FAQ's

  • What methods of payment can I use?

    • As of right now, BTU does not allow payment as a form of trade. In spirit of trades, only communicated items between traders will be accepted.

  • How long do items take to arrive?

    • That is completely up to you and whoever you are trading with! Whether it is an in-person exchange post BTU agreement or you decide to use some shipping platform to deliver the items.

  • How do I know I won't get scammed?

    • During the sign up process, we require every one to verify their account through their Purdue email to make sure they are a student. Any problems associated with scamming can be directly traced back to the student.
