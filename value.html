Value Your Items

How to get started

  1. Upload a picture of your item(s) to be traded (take a picture from every angle!)
  2. Enter information about your specific item in the designated boxes
  3. The system will process both your image and your entered information and assign them a value of 1-10

1-3
Not so valuable

4-6
Worth something

7-10
Valuable

  • Trading is preferred within value range, but if you and a fellow trader think otherwise, no worries!
  • Get to trading!
